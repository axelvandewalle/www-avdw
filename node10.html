Postdoctoral fellow advertisement

The van de Walle group in the School of Engineering at Brown University is seeking candidates for a postdoctoral position in the general area of phase diagram calculations and condensed matter thermodynamics. Excellent knowledge of thermodynamics, electronic structure calculations, alloy theory and computer programming is required. Familiarity with the ATAT software package is a big plus. The specifics of the research project will be tailored to the strengths and interests of the chosen candidate. Interested candidates, should e-mail their CV to avdw@brown.edu .

The position is also advertised on the ATAT forum.

Brown University is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive academic global community; as an EEO/AA employer, Brown considers applicants for employment without regard to, and does not discriminate on the basis of, gender, race, protected veteran status, disability, or any other legally protected status.
